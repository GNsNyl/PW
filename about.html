About me

academic, professional, and ongoing

Liu, Yunsong
刘云松

------

I am Yunsong Liu, an Urban Designer and Data Scientist dedicated to leveraging data to understand and enhance urban dynamics. My expertise lies at the intersection of urban design, data analysis, and smart cities, with a passion for fostering environmental and social sustainability through data-driven interventions.
我是Yunsong Liu，一位致力于利用数据科学来理解和改善城市动态的城市规划师和数据科学家。我的专业背景融合了城市设计、数据分析和智能城市领域的深入研究，我热衷于通过数据驱动的干预措施来促进环境和社会的可持续性。

Currently, I am pursuing a PhD in Urban Analytics and Smart Cities at The University of Hong Kong, focusing on data analysis and urban modeling. Prior to this, I earned a Master of Design Studies from Harvard University, where I concentrated on data science, machine learning, climate justice, and social sustainability. My academic journey also includes a Master of Urban Design with Distinction from the University of Michigan and a Bachelor of Architecture from Tsinghua University in Beijing.
我目前在香港大学攻读城市分析与智能城市博士学位，专注于数据分析和城市建模。在此之前，我在哈佛大学完成了设计研究硕士学位，期间我专注于数据科学、机器学习、气候正义和社会可持续性的研究。我的学术旅程还包括在密歇根大学获得城市设计硕士学位，并以优异成绩毕业于清华大学建筑学学士学位。

My skill set encompasses data science, programming (including Python, SQL, and JavaScript), and data visualization (such as Gephi, GIS, and Cinema4D). I am fluent in English and Mandarin Chinese, enabling me to communicate and collaborate effectively in multicultural environments.
我的技能涵盖了数据科学、编程（包括Python、SQL和JavaScript）以及数据可视化（如Gephi、GIS和Cinema4D）。我精通英语和普通话，这使我能够在多元文化的环境中有效沟通和协作。

My professional experience includes a role as a Data Scientist at CityDNA Technology CO Ltd., where I contributed to urban and carbon consultancy projects for the World Bank, Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank, and Energy Foundation. Additionally, I served as a Co-Curator at the Harvard Kirkland Gallery and a Research Assistant at the University of Michigan, focusing on climate mitigation and adaptation in distressed neighborhoods in São Paulo, Brazil.
我的专业经历包括在CityDNA Technology CO Ltd.担任数据科学家，为世界银行、亚洲基础设施投资银行和能源基金会提供城市和碳咨询项目。此外，我还曾在哈佛大学Kirkland画廊担任联合策展人，以及在密歇根大学担任研究助理，专注于巴西圣保罗市的气候缓解和适应性研究。

My research has been showcased in various academic conferences and exhibitions, including the presentation of my thesis project at the Venice Biennale of Architecture 2021. My academic achievements have been recognized with several scholarships, such as the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme, the HKU Presidential PhD Scholarship, and the Taubman College MUD Fellowship from the University of Michigan.
我的研究成果已在多个学术会议和展览中展出，包括在2021年威尼斯建筑双年展上展示我的论文项目。我的学术成就也得到了认可，获得了包括香港博士研究生奖学金、HKU校长博士奖学金和密歇根大学Taubman学院MUD奖学金在内的多项奖学金。

I am eager to apply my expertise and passion to the fields of urban planning and data science, contributing to the creation of smarter, more sustainable urban environments.
我期待将我的专业知识和热情投入到城市规划和数据科学领域，为创造更加智能、可持续的城市环境做出贡献。
